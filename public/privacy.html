Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

247 Front Runner ("we", "our", "us") operates the 247frontrunner.com website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and password to create your account.

Business Information: Business name, phone number, and auto-reply message templates you configure.

Call and Message Data: When a call is placed to a phone number managed through our service, we collect the caller's phone number, call status (answered or missed), and any text message replies. This data is used to provide the core service of missed call follow-up.

Payment Information: Payment details are processed and stored by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate our missed call text-back service
  • To send automated text messages to callers who miss a call to your business
  • To track leads and call analytics for your dashboard
  • To process subscription payments
  • To communicate with you about your account

3. Text Messaging

Our service sends automated text messages to individuals who call a business phone number and do not receive an answer. These messages are transactional in nature — they are a direct follow-up to the caller's inbound call attempt. Callers consent to receive this message by placing the phone call. Messages are sent from a phone number provisioned through Twilio on behalf of the business.

Message frequency: typically one message per missed call. Message and data rates may apply. Callers may opt out by replying STOP to any message.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following third-party services necessary to operate our platform:

  • Twilio — for phone number provisioning, call handling, and SMS delivery
  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Supabase — for data storage and authentication
  • Vercel — for application hosting

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. Call logs and lead data are retained to provide ongoing analytics. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and access controls to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time through your dashboard settings. To request complete data deletion, contact us at admin@shed-sync.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at admin@shed-sync.com.
